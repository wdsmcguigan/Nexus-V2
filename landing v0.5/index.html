What makes Nexus different

Four things every other email client gets wrong.

Mainstream clients give you a list. Modern clients give you a faster list. Nexus gives you a schema, a database, and the keys.

A real metadata layer

Custom fields (text, number, date, select, person, URL), workflow statuses, multi-style stars, priority ratings, structured notes — indexed and filterable. Not tags. Not folders. A schema you control.

Gmail gives you stars. Superhuman gives you faster stars. Nexus gives you a schema.

Local-first, by design

Your mail lives as .eml files in a visible Maildir folder and a SQLCipher-encrypted SQLite vault on your disk. Quit the app, your data's still there. Open Finder, you can see it.

Not local cache. Not "offline mode." Your disk is the source of truth.

Zero-knowledge sync

XChaCha20-Poly1305 client-side encryption. The optional relay (run it yourself or use ours) sees ciphertext blobs only. No vendor — including us — can read your mail or your annotations.

Sync should be a feature, not a surveillance vector.

No lock-in, no proprietary blobs

Maildir on disk means any IMAP-aware tool can read your mail tomorrow. No Hey-style hostage data, no proprietary export-as-PDF charade. Nexus is a layer on top of standards, not a replacement for them.

The exit door is always open. You'll never need it.

Built for the power user

Three workflows your current email client can't do.

Concrete patterns from real beta users. Each one is hours saved every week.

The personal CRM

Your inbox is already a CRM. Make it act like one.

Add Company, Deal Stage, and Next Touch custom fields. Kanban your pipeline by Deal Stage. Filter Company=Acme AND Status≠Closed and you have an instant account view across years of email.

Replaces: Streak, Salesmate, a manual spreadsheet.
The researcher's vault

Every newsletter, every paper, every link — searchable.

Tag papers and threads by topic. Add a Read boolean and a Notes markdown field. FTS5 searches subject + notes in one query. Beats Notion-clipping every link by a mile.

Replaces: Readwise + Notion + browser bookmarks.
The founder's triage

Inbox-zero as a saved view, not a daily ritual.

Multi-style stars (founder / investor / customer). Filter Priority ≤ 2 AND Status=Open. Kanban by Status to drag through your day. The 200 other messages stay hidden until you ask.

Replaces: Superhuman, the inbox-zero discipline you keep losing.
